These are useful chat commands:

.groupgo	Move group to you
.revive
.tele group <target>	Move group (including you) to target.
.goname <charname>	Teleport to char.
.namego	<charname>	Move char to you.

.gob tar	Gameobject info
.gob add <id> <respawnSecs>	Adds a new gob at your location. respawnSecs must be > 0.
.gob del <guid>	Removes a gob. Useful if you added a bad one.
